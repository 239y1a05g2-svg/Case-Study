About IPL

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is one of the biggest cricket leagues in the world. It was started in 2008 by the BCCI.

IPL is played every year with 10 teams representing different cities of India.

Each team competes to win the IPL trophy.

The league follows T20 format and includes group matches and playoffs.

IPL has helped many young players to showcase their talent.
